yoga in the garden
summer 2019

katy is offering a weekly public class in partnership with active spaces and elizabeth street garden. join katy for an open-level vinyasa practice and support elizabeth street garden in its fight to protect accessible green space. classes are free of charge, and no pre-registration is required. just bring your own mat and come ready to move! class will be held in the garden every sunday at 10:30am from june 2 to september 29.
